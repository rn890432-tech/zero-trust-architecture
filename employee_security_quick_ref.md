# 📘 Employee Security Quick-Reference Guide (2026)

## 1. Your Identity is Your Key 🔑
- **MFA is Mandatory:** Multi-Factor Authentication is required for all company systems. If you receive an MFA prompt you didn't trigger, click "No, it's not me" and alert the SOC immediately.
- **Managed Devices Only:** Always use your company-issued laptop or mobile device for work. Personal devices are blocked from accessing sensitive data to prevent accidental leaks.

## 2. App & Data Permissions 🛡️
- **The "3-Second Rule" for Apps:** Before clicking "Accept" on a pop-up window asking for access to your email or files, ask:
  - Do I know this app?
  - Did I just open it?
  - Does it really need to read my whole mailbox?
- **Approved Apps Only:** If an app is "Unverified," you will see a block screen. Please submit a request via the Help Desk for a security review.

## 3. Working Remotely & Securely 🏠
- **Session Timeouts:** For your protection, systems will occasionally ask you to re-authenticate if you've been inactive or if you've changed locations (e.g., moving from home to a coffee shop).
- **Public Wi-Fi:** Always use the Company VPN when working from public networks.

## 4. Who to Contact 🚨
- **Suspected Phish?** Use the "Report Phishing" button in Outlook/Gmail.
- **Lost Device?** Call the 24/7 Security Hotline at [Insert Phone Number] immediately so we can lock your account and wipe the device remotely.
