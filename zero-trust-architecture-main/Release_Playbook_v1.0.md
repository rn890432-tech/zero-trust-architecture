# Release Playbook v1.0

## Our Release Rhythm: Six Steps

1. **Plan & Scope**
   - Define objectives, deliverables, and timelines.
   - Identify stakeholders and dependencies.

2. **Build & Integrate**
   - Develop features, fixes, and improvements.
   - Integrate with relevant systems and services.

3. **Test & Validate**
   - Run automated and manual tests.
   - Validate compliance, security, and performance.

4. **Document & Communicate**
   - Update release notes, guides, and documentation.
   - Communicate changes to stakeholders.

5. **Deploy & Monitor**
   - Deploy to production or target environment.
   - Monitor for issues and confirm success.

6. **Review & Improve**
   - Gather feedback from team and stakeholders.
   - Analyze outcomes and refine process for next release.

---

## Standards & Best Practices
- Use semantic versioning and conventional commits.
- Ensure accessibility and compliance.
- Document all changes and workflows.
- Encourage feedback for continuous improvement.
